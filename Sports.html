The soccer team that I support is Manchester City, they're a football club based in Manchester,England.

They've won the Premier League three times,in 2012,2014 and most recently in 2018. I hope we will win it again this year but we face considerable pressure from Liverpool FC

Our best players are Sergio Aguero, the record goalscorer for the club, Kevin De Bruyne, one of the most-feared playmakers in world football. More recently,Bernardo Silva has been a revelation, cementing himself as a starter and arguably one of the world's best and most complete footballers
